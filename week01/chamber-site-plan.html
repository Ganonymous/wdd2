Chamber Project Site Plan

Site Name

Camas Chamber of Commerce: Camas is the city this chamber pertains to.

Site Purpose

  • Be a hub to connect and promote local businesses
  • Organize support for business-supporting events and policies

Scenarios

  • How can I send feedback, suggestions, etc. to the chamber?
  • What businesses in Camas have the type of good/service I want to buy/hope to connect with?
  • What events are upcoming that will help grow my business?

Color Schema

Blue #142f4f: Used for the main background

Green #455e22 & Maroon #800000: Backgrounds for individual sections

White #ffffff: Text

Typography

  • Merriweather: Headings, titles, etc.
  • DM Sans: Body text
